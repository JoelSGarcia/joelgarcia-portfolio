Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit https://joelgarcia.me (the “Site”).

2. Personal Information Collected

We do not collect personal information from visitors to the Site. However, we use Google Analytics, which may collect non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser types, referring domains, and other similar data to analyze traffic and improve the Site.

3. Use of Collected Information

The information collected by Google Analytics is used solely for the purpose of analyzing traffic and improving the Site. We do not use this information for any other purpose.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share any personal information with third parties. The non-personal information collected by Google Analytics may be shared with Google as per their privacy policy.

5. Your Choices

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on available at https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at joelgarcia@uiowa.edu.
